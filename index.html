Castoria Seed Quality Standards

Grade A (Premium)

A
  • Size: 8-10 mm length
  • Weight: 14-16g/100 seeds
  • Color: Uniform dark brown
  • Purity: 99%+
  • Germination: 95%+

Best for export & certified planting

Grade B (Standard)

B
  • Size: 7-9 mm length
  • Weight: 12-14g/100 seeds
  • Color: Medium brown
  • Purity: 95-98%
  • Germination: 85-94%

Commercial farming & processing

Grade C (Regular)

C
  • Size: 6-8 mm length
  • Weight: 10-12g/100 seeds
  • Color: Light to mixed brown
  • Purity: 90-94%
  • Germination: 75-84%

Local markets & industrial use
